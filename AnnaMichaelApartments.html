The Tradition of Giving

Puliyampatti St. Antony's Church is a place of miracles. Thousands of devotees visit every Tuesday seeking the Saint's intercession. Offering food (Annadhanam) to the poor and pilgrims is considered one of the most powerful ways to show gratitude for prayers answered.

Charity

Community
